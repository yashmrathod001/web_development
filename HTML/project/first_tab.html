Pizza Recipe

Making a homemade pizza is easy and enjoyable. Start by preheating your oven to **220°C (425°F)**. Place a pizza base on a baking tray and spread about **half a cup of pizza sauce** evenly over the surface. Add your favorite toppings, such as chopped onions, capsicum, sweet corn, mushrooms, or olives. Sprinkle **one cup of mozzarella cheese** generously over the toppings, then season with **oregano** and **chili flakes** for extra flavor. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for **12 to 15 minutes**, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the crust turns golden brown. Remove the pizza from the oven, allow it to cool for **2 to 3 minutes**, then slice it into pieces and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza with your favorite drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • half a cup of pizza sauce
  • one cup of mozzarella cheese
  • chopped onions
  • capsicum
  • sweet corn
  • mushrooms
  • olives
  • oregano
  • chili flakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Place the pizza base on a baking tray.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the surface.
  4. Add your favorite toppings.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the toppings.
  6. Season with oregano and chili flakes.
  7. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.
  8. Allow to cool for 2 to 3 minutes before slicing and serving.
